Structural Health Monitoring (SHM)

Structural health monitoring is a procedure to continuously or periodically assess the safety and integrity of a structural system based on sensing data.
For example, after extreme events like earthquakes, the SHM system can provide a rapid condition assessment of a structure. The SHM system can also detect gradual time-dependent degradation and aging of the system. It has immediate life-saving benefits and market potentials.
