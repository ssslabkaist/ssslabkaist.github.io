Data Analytics

Data analytics is a process of collecting, modeling, and analyzing data to extract insights that support decision-making. In structural health monitoring, data analytics is used for diagnostics and prediction based on regression analysis, neural networks, and big data.
For example, using the acquired data from different conditions of the structure, the data analytics process can classify and quantify the abnormal condition. Also, the data analytics can provide visualization results to the structure manager.
